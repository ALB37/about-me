About Seth Donohue

Who is Seth

I am a creative hands-on engineer with a big picture approach to life, but a detail oriented approach to life's challenges. I chose to follow the hands-on engineering path with a degree in Plastics Engineering and Vehicle design from Western Washintong University in 2009.

In 2015 I took a break from the working world to travel the real world while gathering a new focus on what I'd like to spend my life doing. After almost 2 years of exploring different lands and different cultures I returned to Seattle to develop a new lifestyle and to find a career that would eventually match with that lifestyle.

What Does Seth Do

Seth creates. He creates in code, in metal, in electronics, in art, and in community.

I recently used my coding skills to program arduino micro controllers for pressure-sensistive lightup shoes, which were part of a night costume for Halloween. I also used electronic assembly skills to wire and solder a power system into a jacket and this jacket then powered lightup wires, LEDs, switches, and connectons to power the shoes and lightups pants.

With such sweet shoes and a sweet jacket he needed a fun ride, so why not build a unique bicycle? Using his prototyping, welding and fabrications skills he build a 2 seater side-by-side bicycle for him and a buddy to go on adventures.

What Makes Seth Go

I am driven by big picture challenges that are striving to change the world or just get the creative flow process going. Time doesnt exist when projects are fun!

The above projects are just recent and quick examples of fun things I can pour my energy into. I have previousl worked in an industry that was developing great ideas, but they were moving in a purely profit direction and rarely concerned about the consequences of our consumer driven soceity.
